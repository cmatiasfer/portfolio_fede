Federico Bianchi

Before working independently Federico Bianchi studied the Graphic Design career, obtaining said Title at the University from Buenos Aires (UBA).

Later in 2002 he moved to live in the city of Barcelona, where he worked for nine years in studies of great importance as Dockland, Summa, Ruiz + company, Enric Aguilera Asociados and Zelig Studio. At the same time he collaborated in the Federico Helman Advertising Agency, where he worked in the development of the brand Ciudad de Buenos Aires.

During 2009-2010 he completed a Master in Motion Graphics at the School BAU of Barcelona and at the same time began his journey as a designer freelance working for brands and projects in that city.
Since 2010 he resides again in Argentina, where he carries out different projects in his own studio, either for his own clients in Barcelona and Buenos Aires or collaborating for agencies or studios in both cities.
